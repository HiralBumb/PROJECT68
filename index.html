CHINMAYA VIDYALAYA

Chinmaya Vidyalaya ,Bokaro (Jharkhand) ranked among the top Chinmaya Vidyalayas, who have conceived and implemented the Chinmaya Vision Program successfully into their curriculum. This certificate was a result of the survey and analysis done by CCMT Education Cell, Coimbatore.Chinmaya Vidyalayas are a group of schools operated by the Central Chinmaya Mission Trust (CCMT), founded by Swami Chinmayananda and headed by Swami Tejomayananda. There are more than 80 Chinmaya Vidyalayas across 12 states in India and one in Trinidad. They emphasise the learning of scriptures.
